Install App 📲

Add MindSpace to your home screen for a full-screen, app-like experience — no browser chrome, no address bar.

🤖 Android (Chrome)
1Open MindSpace in Chrome
2Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right)
3Tap "Add to Home screen"
4Confirm — find MindSpace on your home screen 🔮
🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1Open MindSpace in Safari
2Tap the Share ⎙ button (bottom bar)
3Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4Tap Add — done! 🔮
